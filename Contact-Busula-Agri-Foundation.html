Our Location

How to Find Us

Address

123 Mombasa Road, Nairobi, Kenya

Directions

Located 5km from Nairobi city center, near the South B Junction. Look for the green building with "Busula Agri" signage.

Parking

Free parking available on-site for visitors. Additional parking available at the adjacent shopping center.

Public Transportation

Bus routes 34, 45, and 60 stop directly in front of our building. The nearest matatu stage is South B, a 5-minute walk away.

Visit Us

We welcome visitors during our office hours. If you'd like to schedule a tour of our facilities or meet with specific team members, please contact us in advance to arrange an appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What is your typical response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly at +254 712 345 678.

What's the best way to contact you?

For general inquiries, our contact form or email is best. For urgent matters, please call us directly. If you need to speak with a specific department, please use the department-specific email addresses listed above.

Who should I contact in case of an emergency?

For emergencies related to our programs or facilities, please call our emergency hotline at +254 789 012 345, available 24/7. For medical or security emergencies, please contact local emergency services first.

How can I submit documents to you?

Documents can be submitted via email to the appropriate department, or through our secure document upload portal on our website. For physical documents, you can mail them to our office address or deliver them in person during office hours.
