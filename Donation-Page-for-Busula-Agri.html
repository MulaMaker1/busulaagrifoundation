Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating to the Busula Agri Foundation

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, the Busula Agri Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt via email immediately after your donation is processed.

How is my donation used?

82% of your donation goes directly to program services that benefit children and communities, including agricultural training, educational support, and community development initiatives. The remaining funds support essential administrative costs (10%) and fundraising efforts (8%) to sustain our work.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Yes, you can designate your gift to support a specific program area such as Agricultural Education, School Nutrition Program, Community Gardens, or our General Fund. If you choose the General Fund, your donation will be directed to where it's needed most.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. Our donation platform is PCI-compliant and uses 256-bit SSL encryption to ensure your data remains secure.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes, you can choose to make a monthly recurring donation. This provides sustainable support for our programs and helps us plan effectively for the future. You can cancel or modify your recurring donation at any time by contacting our donor services team.

How can I get updates about how my donation is being used?

When you make a donation, you can opt to receive our newsletter which provides regular updates on our programs, success stories, and the impact of donor support. You can also follow us on social media for more frequent updates from the field.
