Our Mission & Vision

Dedicated to creating sustainable solutions that empower vulnerable children and communities through agricultural innovation, education, and holistic support.

Our Mission

To transform the lives of vulnerable children in Africa by providing access to sustainable agricultural knowledge, quality education, and community support systems that foster self-reliance and long-term prosperity.

  • Provide nutritional security through sustainable farming
  • Ensure access to quality education for all children
  • Build resilient community support systems
  • Empower families through skills development

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child in Africa has access to nutritious food, quality education, and the opportunity to thrive in self-sustaining communities that preserve the environment for future generations.

  • Communities free from hunger and malnutrition
  • Children empowered through education and mentorship
  • Sustainable agricultural practices that protect the environment
  • Self-reliant communities that drive their own development

Our Founder's Story

The Busula Agri Foundation was born from Fred Wandela's personal journey and deep commitment to creating sustainable change in vulnerable communities across Africa.

"Growing up in rural Kenya, I witnessed firsthand how agricultural knowledge could transform communities and create pathways out of poverty. Every child deserves access to nutritious food, quality education, and the opportunity to build a better future."
— Fred Wandela, Founder & CEO

After completing his agricultural studies and working with international development organizations, Fred returned to his home region in 2015 with a vision to create a foundation that would address the root causes of child vulnerability through sustainable agriculture and education.

What began as a small community garden project with 15 children has grown into a comprehensive organization serving over 1,200 children across 42 communities, demonstrating the power of sustainable solutions to create lasting change.

Fred Wandela

Founder & CEO

Our Journey

2015

Foundation Established

Fred Fandela established the Busula Agri Foundation with a small community garden project serving 15 children in Kakamega County, Kenya.

2017

First School Partnership

Launched our first school-based agricultural program, integrating sustainable farming education into the curriculum for 120 students.

2019

Community Expansion

Expanded to 15 communities across three counties, reaching over 500 children through agricultural training and educational support.

2021

Training Center Established

Opened our first Agricultural Training Center providing advanced farming techniques, entrepreneurship skills, and mentorship programs.

2023

International Recognition

Received the Sustainable Development Impact Award for our innovative approach to addressing child vulnerability through agriculture.

2025

Reaching New Heights

Currently serving over 1,200 children across 42 communities with comprehensive programs in sustainable agriculture, education, and community development.

Key Program Achievements

Agricultural Innovation

  • Developed 12 drought-resistant crop varieties suitable for local conditions
  • Implemented water-efficient irrigation systems in 28 community gardens
  • Trained 850+ children in sustainable farming techniques
  • Increased crop yields by an average of 65% across partner communities

Educational Impact

  • Improved school attendance rates by 42% in partner communities
  • Provided scholarships to 175 vulnerable children
  • Established 18 school gardens that serve as outdoor classrooms
  • Developed agricultural curriculum adopted by 24 schools

Community Wellbeing

  • Reduced child malnutrition by 38% in target communities
  • Created sustainable income sources for 320+ families
  • Established 15 community seed banks preserving local biodiversity
  • Improved access to clean water for 8,500+ community members

Our Vision for the Future

As we look ahead, the Busula Agri Foundation is committed to expanding our impact and reaching more vulnerable children across Africa. Our strategic plan for the next five years focuses on:

  • Expanding Agricultural Innovation Centers

    Establishing 10 new agricultural training centers to serve as hubs for innovation and knowledge sharing.

  • Strengthening Educational Programs

    Partnering with 50 additional schools to integrate agricultural education into their curriculum.

  • Geographic Expansion

    Extending our programs to three additional countries in East Africa to support more vulnerable children.

  • Research & Development

    Investing in research to develop climate-resilient agricultural methods suitable for changing environmental conditions.
